Our Story

It all started with a simple idea — what if every purchase could leave the planet a little better than before?

As students of Computational Science and technology lovers, we’ve always been fascinated by how innovation can solve everyday problems. But the more we learned, the more we realized that progress means nothing if it harms the environment we depend on.

That’s how EarthHero was born — a community-driven brand that blends sustainability, comfort, and smart design. We believe small actions create big ripples. Every time you buy, earn EcoPoints, or subscribe to a greener habit, you’re helping plant trees, reduce waste, and support fair production.

Our goal isn’t just to sell things — it’s to build a lifestyle that respects both people and the planet. Together, we’re proving that style and sustainability can coexist beautifully.
